About me

School and military service

My name is Avishai Gandelman.
I went to "Tzafit" high school where I majored in History and Chemistry.
After high school I joined a year of volunteer in "Hashomer Hatzair", where I was in charge of a youth club for after school hours, while also helping with regular operations in the school I was working at.
My army service included two years as a combat soldier in various places in Israel.
An additional year in the educational corps, where I operated a youth club and taught lessons in high school.

Employment
